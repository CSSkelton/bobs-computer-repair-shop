  5. Once you have confirm all services required. A new confirmation screen will appear providing a detail record of all"Services and Standard Fees". Please review second image on "Right-Hand side" .


  6. Please make sure the customer is aware of the additional cost of labor"Labor is charge $50.00 per hour".


  7. Cancel button is reserve for the following (anyone no longer interested or mistakes made on the registration form). Once you select the "Cancel".It will void out any previous inputted information.


  8. Before using the confirm button. Please verify all information is valid by reviewing customer personal order form. Once you select the "Confirm Button".It will save all the customer's order under a Bob's Computer Repair Shop account.
