Registration Guide

Customer Profile Information

  1. Ask the customer for their complete first name as it appears on a valid form of identification. Once you obtain this information enter the name in the field labeled "First Name".


  2. Next ask the customer for their complete last name as it appears on a valid form of identification. Once you obtain this information enter the name in the field labeled"Last Name".


  3. Ask the customer for their date of birth. Once you obtain this information. Please, the date of birth in the field labeled "Date of Birth".


  4. Next ask the customer for their full social security number. Advise that this information is used to ensure a valid information is secure with any purchased services they request. Once you obtain this information enter the name in the field labeled "Social Security Number".


  5. Obtain a valid email address. Advise that this information is used to communication a information regarding Bob's Computer Service repair request.Once you obtain this information enter the name in the field labeled "Email Address".


  6. Employee id field should only be enter in for those at that work for Bob's Computer Repair Shop.Once you obtain this information enter the name in the field labeled "Employee id".


  7. Lastly, Obtain a valid mailing address that all communication can be sent to. Avoid any unnecessary abbreviations with street name. Also include in apartment numbers in the field marked "Street Address" Make sure to put valid city name and avoid any suburban names not recognized nationally. Once you obtain this information enter the(street name include house number, city, state, and zip code in the corresponding fields). Example: street address "Street Address" city "City" zip code "Zip Code" state"State".


  8. Cancel button is reserve for the following (anyone no longer interested or mistakes made on the registration form). Once you select the "Cancel".It will void out any previous inputted information.


  9. Before using the submit button. Please verify all information is valid by reviewing customer personal form of identification. Acceptable forms (Driver license, State issued Id, or Military Id). Once you select the "Submit Button".It will save all the customers information under a Bob's Computer Repair Shop account.

Please remember to add only valid information and no special characters
