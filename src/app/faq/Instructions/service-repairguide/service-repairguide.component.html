Service Repair Guide

Additional Service Information

  1. Password reset and Spyware Removal are consider soft services. Expected Time Frame of Completion"1-2 Business Days".

  2. Ram Upgrade and Software Installation are consider standard services. Expected Time Frame of Completion "3-5 Business Days".


  3. PC Tune-Up, Keyboard Cleaning, and Disk Clean-up are all consider Extended Services. Please review "Employee Directory" to schedule an available technician.


  4. All specialty services must be cleared with a manager prior to checkout.


  5. When customers return for pick up of items. Make sure to retrieve the return slip with order number for validation.


  6. All damage or return items must be labeled "Damaged".


  7. Any computers not picked up after "72 hours"must be moved to the storage facility on the second floor. Place the outstanding tickets in the "Red folder".


  8. Customers can not add more work items to orders that have already been completed. A new order form must be done.
